
Step 1: Define Classes
Student Class:

Attributes: id, firstName, lastName, dateOfBirth, address, grades, attendance.
Methods: addGrade(), getGrades(), markAttendance(), getAttendance(), etc.

Grade Class:

Attributes: subject, grade.
Methods: Getters and Setters.

Attendance Class:

Attributes: date, status.
Methods: Getters and Setters.

School Class:

Attributes: List<Student> students.
Methods: addStudent(), removeStudent(), updateStudent(), getStudent(), etc.
