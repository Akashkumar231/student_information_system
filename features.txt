Features:

Student Record Management:
Add, update, delete, and view student records.

Grade Management:
Record and view student grades.

Attendance Management:
Record and view student attendance.

File I/O:
Store student data, grades, and attendance in files.

Serialization:
Serialize and deserialize student records for persistence.

Multithreading:
Handle concurrent access and operations on student records.

OOP:
Design using classes such as Student, Grade, Attendance, and School.
